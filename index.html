Account & Data Deletion
Discipler

If you have an account with Discipler and want to request that your account and associated data be permanently deleted, you may do so at any time by contacting us.

Request Account Deletion

Please email us with the following information:

  • Your full name
  • The email address you used to create your Discipler account

Send your deletion request to:

discipler.app@gmail.com

What Data Will Be Deleted

The following information will be permanently deleted:

  • Your Discipler account
  • Your registered email address
  • Your profile and associated data
  • Any user-generated content stored on our servers

Data That May Be Temporarily Retained

Non-identifiable technical logs may be retained for up to 90 days for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance. These are automatically deleted after the retention period.

Delete Your Account Inside the App

If you have access to the Discipler app, you may also delete your account directly:

  1. Open Discipler
  2. Go to Settings → Account
  3. Tap Delete Account
